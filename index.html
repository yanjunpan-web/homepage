Biography

Yanjun Pan received her M.S. degree and Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Arizona, USA, in 2018 and 2021, respectively. She joined the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Arkansas as an Assistant Professor in 2022. Yanjun Pan's current research interests include cybersecurity, wireless communications, wireless sensing, and network optimization, with emphases on wireless security, 5G-and-beyond communications, mmWave sensing, and cross-layer optimization.

I am looking for Ph.D. and M.S. students who are committed to research. If you are interested, please send me your CV/resume, transcripts, GRE and TOFEL scores, and publications (if any) in one email. Visiting scholars/students are also welcomed.
